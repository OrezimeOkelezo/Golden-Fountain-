About Golden Fountain
Table Water

Golden Fountain Table Water is committed to delivering clean, safe, and refreshing drinking water to homes, offices, and businesses.

We produce premium sachet water, bottled water, and 20L dispenser gallons using multi-stage purification processes that meet NAFDAC standards. Every drop is tested for quality, hygiene, and taste.

With fast and reliable delivery across Bénin City and beyond, our mission is simple: to make access to pure drinking water easy, affordable, and stress-free.

At Golden Fountain, we believe that good health starts with good water. That’s why quality is at the heart of everything we do.
